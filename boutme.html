About

I always feel the world is most honest in silence. In that quiet, textures step forward, the smallest lights begin to matter, and people reveal something true. My practice is not about showing the brightest moments — it is about listening to the dark and recording what answers back.

Behind the Lens

Why dark photography?

Because quiet light reveals truth. Brightness hides; the dark asks questions. I try to answer them with a frame.

What's my process?

I scout for mood—an alley, a window, a face. I wait for light that behaves like a story. Then I listen and press the shutter.
